The Daily Loaf opened in 2022 when former food journalist Sarah Rae traded her press pass for a baker's apron. After years of covering restaurant openings and food trends for the Baltimore Sun, Sarah realized her true passion was creating the stories herself—one loaf at a time. Located just off the Thames Street in Fell's Point, The Daily Loaf brings together Sarah's two loves: storytelling and sourdough. Every morning, we "publish" fresh batches of artisan breads and pastries using traditional techniques and local ingredients. Just like a great news story, our baking is honest, crafted with care, and meant to be shared. Whether you're a student pulling an all-nighter or a professional preparing for morning calls, The Daily Loaf is your daily source for fresh-baked goodness
